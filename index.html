Mission Statement

The mission of the Elm Software Foundation is to promote, protect, and advance the Elm programming language, and to support and facilitate the growth of a diverse and international community of Elm programmers.

Elm Overview

Elm sits at the intersection of web development and academic research on functional programming languages. It has a strong focus on bridging this divide by sharing experience and knowledge between these two very different communities. This communication is hugely valuable to the overall programming community!
