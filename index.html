Elm Software Foundation

Mission Statement

  • Promote, protect, and advance the Elm programming language.
  • Grow a diverse and international community of Elm programmers.

Overview

Elm sits at the intersection of web development and academic research on functional programming languages. It has a strong focus on bridging this divide by sharing experience and knowledge between these two very different communities. This communication is hugely valuable to the overall programming community! For example, Elm has already influenced architecture in React (through Redux and others) and error message quality in Rust.
